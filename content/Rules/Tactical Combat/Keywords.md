##### Accurate
A weapon or ability with this tag makes all attacks with **+1 Boon**.
##### Aegis
**Aegis** is a rare resource that acts as a special shield that negates incoming damage.

Each time a character with **Aegis** takes damage, the damage is reduced to 0 before all other kinds of reduction and the amount of **Aegis** they have is reduced by 1.
##### Armour
When you take damage, you reduce the damage taken by the amount of **Armour** you have, to a minimum of 0.
##### Deadly
A weapon or ability with this tag reduces your **Crit Threshold** by 1 for all attacks made with it.
##### Graze
A weapon or ability with this tag deals damage on a miss as if it had rolled the minimum possible damage it would deal on a hit.
##### Inaccurate
A weapon or ability with this tag makes all attacks with **+1 Bane**.
##### Knockback
A weapon or ability with this tag causes the target to be pushed away from the user a number of spaces equal to the associated value.
##### Persistent Damage
When a character has taken **Persistent Damage**, they immediately take damage equal to the amount of **Persistent Damage**. 

Then, they must roll a **WIT** save at the end of each of their turns. On a failure, they take they take damage equal to the amount of **Persistent Damage**. On a success, they clear all **Persistent Damage**.

When a character takes **Persistent Damage** and is already affected by another source of **Persistent Damage**, the values stack and the highest Save Target is taken.

**Persistent Damage** is unaffected by [[Conditions & Statuses#VULNERABLE|VULNERABLE]], [[Conditions & Statuses#Weak|WEAK]], and [[Keywords#Armour|Armour]].
##### Shield
**Shield** acts as a buffer to your HP. Any damage you take is first dealt to your **Shield**, with any leftover damage being dealt to your HP.

A character can only have **Shield** from once source at a time, and it doesn't stack. If multiple instances of **Shield** would apply, use the one that supplies the largest amount of **Shield**.
