##### Accurate
A weapon with this tag makes all attacks with **+1 Boon**.
##### Aegis
**Aegis** acts as a buffer to your HP. Any damage you take is first dealt to your **Aegis**, with any leftover damage being dealt to your HP.

A character can only have **Aegis** from once source at a time, and it doesn't stack. If multiple instances of **Aegis** would apply, use the one that supplies the largest amount of **Aegis**.
##### Armour
When you take damage, you reduce the damage taken by the amount of **Armour** you have, to a minimum of 0.
##### Deadly
A weapon with this tag reduces your **Crit Threshold** by 1 for all attacks made with it.
##### Graze
A weapon with this tag deals at least an amount of damage equal to the Graze value, regardless if it hits or misses.
##### Inaccurate
A weapon with this tag makes all attacks with **+1 Bane**.
##### Persistent Damage
When a character has taken **Persistent Damage**, they immediately take damage equal to the amount of **Persistent Damage**. 

Then, they must roll a MIGHT or a WIT save (their choice) at the end of each of their turns. On a failure, they take they take damage equal to the amount of **Persistent Damage**. On a success, they clear all **Persistent Damage**.

When a character takes **Persistent Damage** and is already affected by another source of **Persistent Damage**, the values stack and the highest Save Target is taken.

**Persistent Damage** is unaffected by [[Conditions & Statuses#VULNERABLE|VULNERABLE]], [[Conditions & Statuses#Weak|WEAK]], and [[Keywords#Armour|Armour]].
