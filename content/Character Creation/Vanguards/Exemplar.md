#### Core Statistics
| **HP**          | = 24             |
| :-------------- | ---------------- |
| **EVASION**     | = 6 + your Level |
| **HIT**         | = 1 + your Level |
| **SPEED**       | = 5              |
| **Save Target** | = 9 + your Level |
##### Moves
You learn 4 **Moves** of your choice. Each time you gain a Level, you learn an additional **Move**.
##### Exemplar's Armour
You gain an amount of [[Keywords#Armour|Armour]] equal to twice your Tier.
##### Well Trained
You gain one **Combat Talent** of your choice. This Combat Talent doesn't count towards the number of **Combat Talents** you have.
