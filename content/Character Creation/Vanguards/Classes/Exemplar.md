**Description**  
Warriors who master the technique of battle find themselves adorned with the title **Exemplar**. They are adaptable, knowledgeable, and above all else, *perfect*.

**Playstyle**  
Exemplars switch between offensive and defensive playstyles and come with a wider list of Moves. Exemplars are therefore prepared to enter any battle that comes their way. 

**Complexity:** ★★★★★

#### Moves
You learn 4 **Moves** of your choice. Each time you gain a Level, you learn an additional **Move**.
#### Features
##### Exploit Opening
Once per round, when an enemy is within reach of your *Basic Attack* and attempts to move out of reach, you may spend a Reaction to immediately make a *Basic Attack* against them.
##### Exemplar's Armour
You gain an amount of [[Keywords#Armour|Armour]] equal to your Tier.
##### Stances
During combat, you switch between styles known as **Stances**. You begin combat **Stanceless**, meaning you benefit from none of your **Stances**. You can only be in one of your **Stances** at a time. Each time you enter a **Stance**, you stay in it until you enter a new one or until the end of your next turn.

Your **Stances** are listed below.

**OFFENSIVE STANCE**  
You enter your **Offensive Stance** after using a **Move** that can deal damage.

While in your **Offensive Stance**, you gain a **+1 Bonus** to your **HIT** and you reduce your **Crit Threshold** by 1.

**DEFENSIVE STANCE**  
You enter your **Defensive Stance** after using a **Move** that cannot deal damage.

While in your **Defensive Stance**, you increase your **Armour** by your Tier and your **Evasion** by 1.

**EXEMPLARY STANCE**  
If you are in your **Offensive Stance** and trigger an effect that would put you in your **Defensive Stance**, or vice versa, you instead enter your **Exemplary Stance**. 

While in your **Exemplary Stance**, you gain the benefits of both your **Offensive Stance** and your **Defensive Stance**.
