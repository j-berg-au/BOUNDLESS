When creating a Vanguard, select one weapon from the list below. You are encouraged to rename and re-flavour a weapon to better suit your character if you so desire.
##### Ironblade
*Melee Attack, Reach 1*  
**Damage:** 1d12+1 || 2d12+2 || 3d12+3  
##### Longspear
*Melee Attack, Reach 2*  
**Damage:** 1d6+1 || 2d6+2 || 3d6+3  
##### Scythe
*Melee Attack, Reach 1*  
*[[Keywords#Graze|Graze]]*  
**Damage:** 1d10+1 || 2d10+2 || 3d10+3  
##### Silverblade
*Melee Attack, Reach 1*  
*[[Keywords#Accurate|Accurate]]*  
**Damage:** 1d10+1 || 2d10+2 || 3d10+3  
##### Sword & Board
*Melee Attack, Reach 1*  
**Damage:** 1d6+1 || 2d6+2 || 3d6+3  
At the start of each of your turns, you gain 1 || 2 || 3 [[Keywords#Shield|Shield]].
##### Warhammer
*Melee Attack, Reach 1*  
*[[Keywords#Inaccurate|Inaccurate]], [[Keywords#Deadly|Deadly]]*  
**Damage:** 2d6+1 || 4d6+2 || 6d6+3  
##### Your Bare Hands
*Melee Attack, Reach 1*  
**Damage:** 1d4+1 || 2d4+2 || 3d4+3  
When you hit with this weapon as part of a *Basic Attack*, it deals its listed damage twice (this alters how it interacts with things such as [[Keywords#Aegis|Aegis]], [[Keywords#Armour|Armour]], and some Conditions). When you use this weapon to make any other form of *Attack* (for example, as part of a **Move**), it instead deals **2d4+1 || 4d4+2 || 6d4 + 3** once.
