#### Core Statistics
| **HP**          | = 6               |
| :-------------- | ----------------- |
| **EVASION**     | = 14 + your Level |
| **HIT**         | = Your Level      |
| **SPEED**       | = 5 + your Tier   |
| **Save Target** | = 10 + your Level |
##### Tricks
You learn 3 **Tricks** of your choice. Every odd level, you learn a new **Trick**. Additionally, each time you increase in Tier you may learn an additional **Trick**.
##### Unseen
Once on each of your turns, you may [[Your Turn#Hide|Hide]] as a Free Action, doing so before taking any other actions.
##### Elusive
You gain **+1 Boon** to all saves.
