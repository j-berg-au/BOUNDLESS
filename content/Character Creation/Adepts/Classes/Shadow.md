**Description**  
Masters of stealth and edge, **Shadows** abuse their ability to go unnoticed and hit their enemies where it hurts most.

**Playstyle**  
Shadows shine brightest when enveloped in darkness. To be most effective, make use of the [[Conditions & Statuses#Invisible|INVISIBLE]] and [[Conditions & Statuses#Hidden|HIDDEN]] statuses as much as possible. 

**Complexity:** ★★☆☆☆

| **Core Statistcs** |                   |
| :----------------- | ----------------- |
| **HP**             | = 6               |
| **EVASION**        | = 14 + your Level |
| **HIT**            | = Your Level      |
| **SPEED**          | = 5 + your Tier   |
| **Save Target**    | = 9 + your Level  |
#### Tricks
You learn 3 **Tricks** of your choice. Every odd level, you learn a new **Trick**. Additionally, each time you increase in Tier you may learn an additional **Trick**.
#### Features
##### Elusive
You gain **+1 Boon** to all **FINESSE**, **WIT**, and **PRESENCE** saves.
##### Unseen
At the end of each of your turns, flip a coin and call it. If you guess correctly, you become [[Conditions & Statuses#Invisible|INVISIBLE]] until the end of your next turn.
