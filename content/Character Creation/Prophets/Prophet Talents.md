## Combat Talents
### Tier I
##### Condemn
As a Minor Action, you may inflict the [[Conditions & Statuses#Marked|MARKED]] condition on a target within Range 10.
##### Divine Protection
When you use a Miracle on an ally and gain the Divine Effect, both you and the target gain [[Keywords#Aegis|Aegis]] equal to your Level.
##### Guidance
When you take the [[Your Turn#Set-Up|Set-Up]] Minor Action, you may instead choose an ally within Range 5.
##### Inspired Action
When you use your *Basic Attack*, you may forego the attack to allow an ally within Range 8 to make a single *Basic Attack* instead as a Reaction.
##### Twinned Totem
*Prerequisite: Druid*  
You may have two Totems active at a given time, but they must be active on different characters.
##### Protective Presence
You an all allies within Range 3 gain **+1 Boon** on all saves.
