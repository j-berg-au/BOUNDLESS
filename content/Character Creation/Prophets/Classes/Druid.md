**Description**  
Heralds of the natural world, **Druids** call for spirits, plants, and animals to aid their allies and hinder their foes.

**Playstyle**  
Druids use their Feathered Friend to deliver Miracles from safety. An effective Druid is essentially present in two places at once.

**Complexity:** ★★☆☆☆

#### Miracles
You learn 3 **Miracles** of your choice. Each time you increase in Tier, you learn an additional 4 **Miracles**.
#### Features
##### Gifts of the Earth
You gain the **Miracles** *[[Miracles#EAGLE TOTEM|Eagle Totem]]* and *[[Miracles#Ursine Totem|Ursine Totem]]* for free, without them counting towards the number of **Miracles** you know.
##### Feathered Friend
When combat starts, you summon a fae bird to your aid. The bird appears in a space adjacent to you and has the following statistics. 

| **Feathered Friend Statistics** |                   |
| :------------------------------ | ----------------- |
| **HP**                          | = 1               |
| **EVASION**                     | = 14 + your Level |
| **SPEED**                       | = 5 + your Tier   |
| **MIGHT save**                  | = -5 + your Level |
| **FINESSE save**                | = 4 + your Level  |
| **WIT save**                    | = Your Level      |
| **PRESENCE save**               | = 1 + your Level  |

Your bird can use a **Standard Move** on your turn, but otherwise takes no actions. You can use your **Miracles** as if you were in your bird's space.  

If your bird is [[Conditions & Statuses#DEFEATED|DEFEATED]], it immediately vanishes. You can call upon your bird again as a Minor Action.
