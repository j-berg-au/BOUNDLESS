**Description**  
Worshipers of the Gods, **Priests** act as a channel for their deities to express their power throughout the world.

**Playstyle**  
Priests support their allies, building Favour in the process. With enough Favour, they can call for a powerful attack through their God. Support. Smite. Repeat.

**Complexity:** ★☆☆☆☆

#### Miracles
You learn 3 **Miracles** of your choice. Each time you increase in Tier, you learn an additional 4 **Miracles**.
#### Features
##### Temple's Teachings
You gain the **Miracles** *[[Miracles#CLEANSE|Cleanse]]* and *[[Miracles#REVITALISE|Revitalise]]* for free, without them counting towards the number of **Miracles** you know.
##### Favour of the Gods
Whenever you use a **Miracle** on an ally, you gain **1 Favour**. When you reach **5 Favour**, you may spend all your **Favour** to use the *Smite the Heretical* action below. You lose all Favour when combat ends.
##### Smite the Heretical
*Major Action*  
Choose a target within Range 10. They immediately take 10 || 20 || 30 damage and are [[Conditions & Statuses#MARKED|MARKED]] and [[Conditions & Statuses#SLOWED|SLOWED]] until the end of your next turn. Additionally, the target must make a **PRESENCE** save. On a failed save, roll 1d6 to determine an additional effect: 

| d6  | Effect                                                                                                  |
| :-: | ------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
|  1  | [[Conditions & Statuses#IMMOBLISED\|IMMOBLISED]] until the end of your next turn.                       |
|  2  | [[Conditions & Statuses#BLINDED\|BLINDED]] until the end of your next turn.                             |
|  3  | [[Conditions & Statuses#VULNERABLE\|VULNERABLE]] (value equal to Tier) until the end of your next turn. |
|  4  | [[Conditions & Statuses#WEAK\|WEAK]] (value equal to Tier) until the end of your next turn.             |
|  5  | [[Conditions & Statuses#STUNNED\|STUNNED]]                                                              |
|  6  | The target takes additional [[Keywords#Persistent Damage\|Persistent Damage]] equal to your Tier.       |
