##### Spells
You learn 4 **Spells** of your choice, and an additional **Spell** each time you gain a Level.
##### Forbidden Deals
As an Invoker, you negotiate with unseen magical entities for your power. As a Free Action at the start of any of your turns, you may make one of the following **Forbidden Deals**.

**EYE FOR AN EYE**  
Choose a character within Range 5. They must make a **PRESENCE** save or become [[Conditions & Statuses#BLINDED|BLINDED]] until the start of your next turn. Regardless of the outcome, you are [[Conditions & Statuses#BLINDED|BLINDED]] until the start of your next turn.

**SANGUINE SECRETS**  
You gain an amount of **Mana** of your choice, up to your Level. Until the start of your next turn, you are [[Conditions & Statuses#VULNERABLE|VULNERABLE]] with value equal to the **Mana** gained.

**STOLEN SWIFTNESS**  
You may take an additional Minor Action on this turn. As soon as this turn ends, you are [[Conditions & Statuses#STUNNED|STUNNED]].
