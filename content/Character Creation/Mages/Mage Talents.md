## Combat Talents
### Tier I
##### Arcane Distance Theorem
All **Spells** you know have their Range increased by 2, provided that their Range was not initially 0.
##### Assimilate Elements
*Prerequisite: Conduit*  
Choose one of the following tags: Fire, Ice, Stone, Lightning. Any **Spell** you know that has one of these tags but not your chosen tag is transformed, having that tag swapped for your chosen tag. 
##### Further Study
You learn two additional **Spells** of your choice.
##### Mana Sink
While you have an amount of **Mana** less than or equal to three times your Tier, your *Basic Attack* deals an additional 1d6 damage on a hit.
##### Rest the Aether
When you finish one of your turns on which you did not spend any **Mana** or use a *Basic Attack*, you regain **Mana** equal to twice your Tier.
##### Siphon
When you hit with your *Basic Attack*, you regain **Mana** equal to your Tier.
### Tier II
##### Dual Domain
*Prerequisite: Conduit, Tier II*  
Select an additional **Magical Domain**, gaining its **Spells** and feature.
##### Unleash Arcane
*Prerequisite: Conduit, Tier II*  
Activate as Free Action. Two rounds of ABSOLUTE POWER. One round of losing your turn 
- Make this a talent tree, with options for each **Magical Domain**
### Tier III

##### Master of Three
*Prerequisite: Conduit, Tier III, Dual Domain*  
Select an additional **Magical Domain**, gaining its **Spells** and feature.
