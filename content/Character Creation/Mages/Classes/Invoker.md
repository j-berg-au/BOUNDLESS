**Description**  
Practitioners of the occult, **Invokers** forge contracts with unseen beings of immense magical power.

**Playstyle**  
Invokers balance the curses of their Forbidden Deals with the blessings they grant in order to claw for power otherwise unattainable. An effective Invoker knows how to mitigate such curses.

**Complexity:** ★★★☆☆

#### Spells
You learn 4 **Spells** of your choice, and an additional **Spell** each time you gain a Level.
#### Features
##### Forbidden Deals
As an Invoker, you negotiate with unseen magical entities for your power. As a Free Action at the start of any of your turns, you may make one of the following **Forbidden Deals**.

**SANGUINE SECRETS**  
You gain an amount of **Mana** of your choice, up to your Level. Until the end of your next turn, you are [[Conditions & Statuses#VULNERABLE|VULNERABLE]] with value equal to the **Mana** gained.

**STOLEN SWIFTNESS**  
You may take an additional Minor Action on this turn. As soon as this turn ends, you are [[Conditions & Statuses#STUNNED|STUNNED]].
