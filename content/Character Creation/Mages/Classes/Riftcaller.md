**Description**  
Mages who bind other creatures to serve them, calling the through rifts in the Aether, are known as **Riftcallers**. These creatures could be undead, animals, spirits, elementals, or any other kind of creature.

**Playstyle**  
A Riftcaller summons creatures to fight alongside them, distracting enemies, and taking hits. A good Riftcaller will always have a Minion beside them, ready to take a hit for their master.

**Complexity:** ★★★★★

#### Spells
You learn 4 **Spells** of your choice, and an additional **Spell** each time you gain a Level.
#### Features
##### Summon Minion
You gain the ability to call forth a creature to fight alongside you. As a Minor Action, you may spend **5 Mana** to summon two [[Minion|Minions]], each in an unoccupied space within Range 5. You may only have a number of **Minions** active equal to twice your Tier or lower.

As a Minor Action, you may command up to two **Minions** to take a turn immediately, dictating their actions. If you do not take this Minor Action, the **Minions** will not act for that round, instead simply waiting around for you to tell them what to do.

The **Minions** disappear at the end of combat, or when they are [[Conditions & Statuses#DEFEATED|DEFEATED]].
##### Minion Shield
Whenever you would take damage while adjacent to one of your **Minions**, you may instead redirect that damage to your adjacent **Minion**.
