**Description**  
Scholars of magic, **Savants** dedicate their lives to understanding and manipulating the aether. They are lifelong learners with a drive to understand the world around them.

**Playstyle**  
Savants boast the widest selection of Spells of any Mage, granting them a level of flexibility otherwise unattainable. 

**Complexity:** ★★☆☆☆

| **Core Statistics** |                   |
| :------------------ | ----------------- |
| **HP**              | = 6               |
| **EVASION**         | = 8 + your Level  |
| **HIT**             | = Your Level      |
| **SPEED**           | = 4               |
| **Save Target**     | = 11 + your Level |
#### Spells
You learn 5 **Spells** of your choice. Each time you gain a Level, you learn another **Spell**. Additionally, each time you increase in Tier, you learn an additional **Spell**.
#### Features
##### Well-Learned
You gain one **Combat Talent** of your choice. This Combat Talent doesn't count towards the number of **Combat Talents** you have.
