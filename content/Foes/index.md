### Grunt
|   HP    |      1      |
| :-----: | :---------: |
| EVASION | = 5 + Level |
|  SPEED  |      4      |
##### Coordination
Two **Grunts** can take their turn at the same time.
##### Clumsy
A **Grunt** automatically fails all saves.
##### Attack!
*Major Action*
The **Grunt** deals **Td4** damage to a character within Range 5.
