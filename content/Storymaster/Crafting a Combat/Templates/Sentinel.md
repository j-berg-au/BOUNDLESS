##### Exploit Opening
Whenever a character within Reach 2 of the **Sentinel** attempts to voluntarily move outside Reach 2, the **Sentinel** may use their **Swing** against the character as a Reaction.

### Actions
##### Pummel
*Major Action, [[Keywords#Inaccurate|Inaccurate]], [[Keywords#Graze|Graze]]*  
The **Sentinel** makes an attack against a character within Reach 2. On a hit, the target takes **X/X/X damage** and must succeed on a **MIGHT** save or become [[Conditions & Statuses#Stunned|STUNNED]].
##### Raise Shield
*Minor Action*  
The **Sentinel** prepares itself for incoming fire, granting itself **1/2/3** [[Keywords#Aegis|Aegis]].
##### Swing
*Minor Action*  
The **Sentinel** makes an attack against a character within Reach 2. On a hit, the target takes **X/X/X damage**.
