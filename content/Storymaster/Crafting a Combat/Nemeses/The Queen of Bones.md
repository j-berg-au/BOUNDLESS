##### Nemesis
**The Queen of Bones** can take a number of turns in a round equal to the number of PCs.
##### Mortum Esoterica
*Automatically triggers at the end of Round 3*  
**The Queen of Bones** summons a size 2 **Totem of Hatred** for each of the PCs. 

Ask each player, *"What does your character hate most about themselves?"*. Their corresponding **Totem of Hatred** appears within Range 3 of them and is a sculpture made of bones depicting their character displaying this quality.

Each **Totem of Hatred** has **15 || 30 || 45 HP**, and any PC becomes [[Conditions & Statuses#Weak|WEAK]] 3 || 6 || 9 while within Range 3 of their corresponding Totem.

If a **Totem of Hatred** remains undestroyed by the end of Round 6, the PC the structure resembles dies immediately. Each PC becomes aware of their impending doom upon the summoning of the Totem.

### Actions
##### Bone Spears
*Major Action*  
**The Queen of Bones** summons spears from the earth around her in all spaces within Range 2. Any character within this area must make a **FINESSE** save or take **2d6 || 4d6 || 6d6 damage**.
##### Decomposition Ray
*Major Action*  
**The Queen of Bones** launches a ray of sickly energy, making an attack against a target within Range 10. On a hit, the target takes **3d4 || 6d4 || 9d4 damage** and becomes [[Conditions & Statuses#Slowed|SLOWED]] as their bones begin to decompose.
##### Skeletal Summons (1/round, 3/scene)
*Major Action*  
**The Queen of Bones** summons four skeletal [[Minion|Minions]] within Range 5. Whenever a character is targeted by one of these Minions' "Attack!" action, they become [[Conditions & Statuses#Marked|MARKED]]. 
##### Voodoo (2/scene)
*Major Action*  
**The Queen of Bones** creates a small Voodoo Doll of a character they have seen during this scene. The target must succeed a **PRESENCE** save or the Voodoo Doll is tethered to their being.

Once tethered, **The Queen of Bones** may now force the target to move up to their **SPEED** and make a *Basic Attack* against a target of her choice. She may repeat this as a Minor Action on her turns until the end of the Round.

### Amplifiers
- When using Mortom Esoterica, they self-heal.
- Bonus spells
- Sacrifice a Minion to do a cool thing
- Expend MARKED on a target to inflict another condition, no save.
