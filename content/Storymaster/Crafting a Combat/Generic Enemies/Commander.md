##### Redirect
Anytime the **Commander** would take damage, an adjacent ally of the **Commander** takes the damage in its place.

---
### Actions
##### Hit Them, Damn It!
*Minor Action*  
The **Commander** instructs an ally of theirs within Range 5 to use their Reaction to make either a Melee Attack or a Ranged Attack they are capable of taking as a Minor Action. This attack must be normally usable as a Minor Action.

On a hit, this attack deals an additional **1d6 || 2d6 || 3d6 damage.**
##### Formation!
*Major Action*  
The **Commander** instructs any number of their allies within Range 8 to move up to their **SPEED** as a Reaction.

---
### Amplifiers
##### Summon
*Major Action*  
The **Commander** summons two [[Minion|Minions]] within Range 5.
