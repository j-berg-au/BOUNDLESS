##### Coordination
Two **Minions** can take their turn at the same time.
##### Clumsy
A **Minion** automatically fails all saves.
##### Tunnel Vision
Any Minor Actions a **Minion** takes counts as taking a Major Action.

---
### Actions
##### Attack!
*Major Action, Ranged Attack, Range 4*  
Damage on hit: **3 || 6 || 9**. 

---
### Amplifiers
##### Exploding
When the Minion is [[Conditions & Statuses#Defeated|DEFEATED]], they explode in a Size 2 Circle. All characters within the explosion must pass a **FINESSE** save or take **1d6 || 2d6 || 3d6** damage, taking half as much on a success.
