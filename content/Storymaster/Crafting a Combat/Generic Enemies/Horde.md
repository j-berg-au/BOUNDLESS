##### Diminishing
When the **Horde's** **HP** is less than half its maximum, its size is reduced to 2 and its **Many Attacks** deals its damage twice instead of four times. 
##### Many Folk
The **Horde** takes double damage from effects acting on multiple spaces the **Horde** occupies.

---
### Actions
##### Envelop
*Minor Action*  
The **Horde** envelops an adjacent character smaller than it. The **Horde** moves so that the target is within the area the **Horde** occupies.  

While enveloped, the target becomes [[Conditions & Statuses#Immobilised|IMMOBILISED]], [[Conditions & Statuses#Off-Guard|OFF-GUARD]], and [[Conditions & Statuses#Blinded|BLINDED]]. The target may attempt to end with a successful **FINESSE** save as a Minor Action, moving into a space adjacent to the **Horde**.
##### Many Attacks
*Minor Action, Melee Attack, Reach 1*  
If the target of this attack is enveloped (as above), the attack is made with **+1 Boon**. On a hit, the target takes **1d4 || 2d4 || 3d4** damage four times.
