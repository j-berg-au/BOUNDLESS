##### Phase
The **Constructor** can move through walls, but cannot end its movement within one. If it tries to end its movement within a wall, it returns to the space from which it entered a wall.

---
### Actions
##### Build Wall
*Minor Action*  
The **Constructor** creates a wall in an unoccupied space adjacent to it, serving as **hard cover**. This wall has **5 || 10 || 15 HP**, **0 EVASION**, and automatically fails all saves except **MIGHT** saves, in which it has a score of **+2 || +6 || +10**.
##### Alter Terrain
*Minor Action*  
The **Constructor** places a Size 3 Square within Range 5. The selected area becomes **Slowing Terrain**.
##### Throw Brick
*Minor Action, Ranged Attack, Range 5*  
On hit: **1d6 || 2d6 || 3d6** damage.

---
### Amplifiers
##### Construction Cloud
*Minor Action*  
The **Constructor** places a Size 2 Circle within Range 8. A fog fills this area, which may be used as **soft cover**.
##### Danger! Keep Out!
*Minor Action*  
The **Constructor** chooses a space within Range 8. This space becomes a **Hazard**, dealing **2 || 4 || 6 damage**.
##### Renovations
*Minor Action*  
The **Constructor** may choose a wall adjacent to it, and move it to another unoccupied space adjacent to it.
