All consumables require a Minor Action to use. You may only use the consumable on yourself unless the consumable has the *Splash* tag, in which case you may administer it to another character within Range 3. If that character is unwilling, they may avoid the effect with a successful **FINESSE** save.

Unless otherwise stated, all consumables are expended after a single use.
